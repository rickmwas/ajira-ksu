# Project Overview - Ajira Club Kisii University (Ajira Club KSU)

## What is Ajira Club KSU?
Ajira Club Kisii University is a digital training, peer mentorship, and online work enablement platform. It serves as a campus chapter of Kenya's National Ajira Digital Program (sponsored by the Ministry of ICT, KEPSA, and eMobilis) to empower university students with the skills and access required to thrive in the online gig economy.

## System Goals
1. **Education & Training**: Provide peer-led learning cohorts with high-quality, practical training tracks.
2. **Opportunities Board**: Connect students with verified freelance contracts, internships, scholarships, and hackathons.
3. **Events & Workshops**: Coordinate physical and digital workshops, registration, and attendance tracking on campus.
4. **Official Certification**: Vette, generate, and print certificates of completion for certified digital skill tracks.
5. **Member & Role Management**: Maintain student registry and support role-based access control (RBAC).

## User Roles
* **Guest (Public)**: Unauthenticated visitor accessing general club information, programs list, events, success stories, and registration.
* **Member (Student Portal)**: Authenticated student learning via courses, completing modules, registering for events, applying for opportunities, and printing verified certificates.
* **Executive (Club Leader)**: Authenticated leadership role with capabilities to manage club events, verify attendance, post announcements, and update resources.
* **Admin (System Administrator)**: Authenticated admin managing user accounts, editing roles, modifying courses, analyzing dashboards, and running site audits.

## MVP Scope
* **Public Website**: Marketing presence (`ajiraksu.org` equivalent path) showing About, Programs, Events, Leadership, Blog, Gallery, and Contacts.
* **Student Portal**: Dashboard, Profile management, and track listings.
* **Learning Management System (LMS)**: Course reader with markdown curriculum content, progress indicator, and persistent completion checklists.
* **Events & Attendance System**: View upcoming events, RSVP, and check attendance metrics.
* **Certificates Engine**: Landscape A4 printable template featuring verified serial codes and digital signatures.
* **Opportunities Board**: Curated lists of internships and digital jobs.

## Out of Scope (Explicitly Excluded)
* Online payments, fees, or gateway subscriptions.
* Advertisements or sponsor placement monetization systems.
* Multi-university federation (designed strictly for Kisii University chapter for now).
* Mobile application (web application is mobile-responsive).
